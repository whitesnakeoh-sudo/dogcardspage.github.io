§ 01

Overview & Philosophy

At Dog Cards, we believe your privacy matters. We are not a data company. We have no advertisers. We do not monetize user information. The only data we collect is the minimum required to operate a functional card-collecting bot — things like knowing who owns which cards and being able to resolve disputes about who grabbed something first.

We are transparent about what we collect, why we collect it, and how it's used. This document exists to give you that transparency in full detail. We've written it in plain, honest language because we respect your intelligence and your right to know exactly what you're agreeing to.

🌟 Our Core Promise

We strictly obey United States law and Discord's Terms of Service. We will never collect anything Discord does not permit. We will never sell your data. And if you want your data gone entirely — we will make that happen, fully and immediately, no questions asked.

§ 06

Console Logging Explained

When you use the Dog Cards Bot, your Discord username and User ID may appear in the Bot's console output. This is a standard practice for Discord bots — it allows the developers and operators to see who is actively using the Bot, monitor for unusual activity, and debug technical issues.

🖥️ What Console Logging Means

Console logs are internal operational records. They are visible only to the Dog Cards team. They are not published, shared, or accessible to other users. Think of it like a server access log — something a website operator might use to see who's visiting their site.

Console logs are not permanent records. They are session-based and exist only as long as the Bot is running. On restart, console log history is cleared. If you opt out of data collection (see Section 9-10), your username and ID will no longer appear in any console output after the next Bot restart.

✅ After Opt-Out

If you choose to opt out, your name and Discord ID will be completely invisible in the console. The team will never see your username or know you are using the Bot. On the next Bot restart, all existing log references to your account are cleared permanently.

§ 09

Your Rights & Opt-Out

You have rights over your own data. We respect those rights completely. Even though the data we collect is basic and not privacy-obstructing, we recognize that some users simply prefer to have no data collected — and we honor that preference unconditionally.

👁️
Right to Know

You have the right to ask what data we hold about you. Open a support ticket and we will tell you exactly what's stored.

🗑️
Right to Deletion

You can request that all data associated with your Discord account be permanently deleted. We will comply fully on the next Bot restart.

🚪
Right to Opt Out

You can opt out of all data collection at any time. After opt-out, your account is invisible to the Bot and our team.

🔄
Right to Opt Back In

Changed your mind? You may opt back in at any time. Data collection resumes from the moment of opt-in.

💬 We Respect You

We want to be honest: the data we collect is basic, and isn't designed to hinder or invade your privacy. But if you want out, we will honor that request completely and without complaint. We respect our users and their lives.

§ 10

Opt-Out Process in Detail

Here is exactly what happens when you request a data opt-out, step by step:

Step 1 — Open a Support Ticket

Join the Official Dog Cards Discord server. Navigate to #support and open a new ticket. Simply state that you wish to opt out of data collection.

Step 2 — Team Acknowledges Your Request

A member of the Dog Cards team will acknowledge your request. We will confirm what data is associated with your account at that time.

Step 3 — Acknowledge the Trade-Off

We will inform you that opting out means timestamp data proving card grabs will no longer be recorded. You must confirm you understand and accept this — that disputes about card ownership cannot be resolved in your favor without that data.

Step 4 — Data Removed on Next Restart

On the next Bot restart, all data tied to your account is permanently removed. Your name and Discord ID will no longer appear anywhere in our systems or console logs.

Step 5 — Invisible Going Forward

From that point on, the Bot has no awareness of your account. You may still use the Bot, but no data is stored and no ownership records are maintained for your account.

⚠️ Important Acknowledgement

By completing the opt-out process, you explicitly agree that: (1) no timestamp data will be maintained for your future card grabs, (2) ownership disputes involving your account cannot be resolved using data-based proof, and (3) you accept these limitations willingly and without duress. We will 100% honor your request — but we want you to make an informed choice.
