## ✨ What is Dog Cards?

> A fun, collectible card bot for Discord communities built around a love of dogs. Cards spawn in eligible channels, and the **first person to claim one owns it**. Simple, fast, and fair.

- 🏆 **First-Grab Ownership** — Whoever grabs a card spawn first is the rightful owner, verified by timestamped logs
- 🖥️ **Server Autonomy** — Server owners set their own rules (trading, stealing, gifting — their call)
- 🔍 **Proof System** — All disputes resolved using objective, timestamped server-side data
- 🔒 **Privacy Respecting** — Minimal data collection, full opt-out support, zero monetization of your info

### § 01 · Acceptance of Terms

> ℹ️ **Plain English:** Using the bot means you agree to play fair. If you can't agree to fair play, don't use the bot — simple as that.

By using the Bot in any Discord server, you agree to these Terms in full. Continued use after updates means renewed acceptance.

### § 02 · What is Dog Cards

The **Official Dog Cards Group** is the primary server run directly by the team — rules there match these Terms exactly. Any **third-party server** running the Bot is a separate entity and may run its own rules (see §04).

### § 03 · Core Rules — Card Grabbing

> 🏆 **The Golden Rule:** *The first person to claim a card owns that card.*

- 🏆 **First-Grab Ownership** — Timestamps are the definitive source of truth in all disputes
- 🚫 **No Card Stealing** — Prohibited in the Official Group via exploit, hack, or manipulation
- ⚡ **Fair Competition** — No bots, scripts, or automation; everyone gets an equal shot
- 🔎 **Proof-Based Disputes** — Verbal claims aren't enough; server logs decide

> ⚠️ **Important:** Trying to claim someone else's card — or forcing a trade through intimidation or exploitation — is a Terms violation regardless of intent.

### § 04 · Server-Specific Rules

- 🖥️ **Server Owner Rights** — You are **100% in control** of your own server's rules. Allow trading, gifting, even stealing if you want; that's your call.
- 📋 **Your Rules, Your Call** — Your server's mechanics are valid within your server. Dog Cards won't override them.
- 🤝 **We Honor Your Authority** — Server-level decisions are treated as legitimate within your community.
- 🔀 **Independent Context** — Your server's rules don't transfer to the Official Group, and vice versa.
- ⚖️ **Discord TOS Supersedes All** — No server rule may ever override Discord's own Terms of Service.

### § 05 · Prohibited Conduct

**❌ Never Allowed**
- Automated scripts/bots to grab cards
- Exploiting bugs or glitches
- Harassment over cards
- Impersonating staff
- Hacking the Bot
- False ownership claims
- Account sharing for advantage
- Circumventing opt-outs

**✅ Always Permitted**
- Grabbing cards manually, fair and fast
- Trading consensually
- Requesting support via ticket
- Suggesting features
- Opting out of data collection
- Running the bot on your own server
- Setting your own server rules
- Disputing grabs with evidence

### § 06 · Proof & Dispute System

All grabs are timestamped and tied to Discord User IDs — objective data, no subjectivity. To dispute a grab, open a ticket in `#support` with the card name, approximate time, and your username.

> ⚠️ **Opt-Out Limitation:** If you've opted out of data collection, there's no timestamp proof available for your grabs, meaning disputes involving your account may not be resolvable in your favor. You must acknowledge this trade-off before opting out.

- ⚠️ **Trade-Off** — No more grab-proof; you forfeit dispute-winning ability going forward
- 🔄 **Reversible** — Opt back in anytime via another ticket

> *"We respect you and your life choices."* Honored 100%, no questions asked.

### § 01 · Overview & Philosophy

> 🌟 **Our Core Promise:** We strictly obey U.S. law and Discord's ToS. We never collect anything Discord doesn't permit. We never sell your data. And if you want it gone, it's gone — fully, immediately, no questions asked.

We're not a data company. No advertisers. No monetization of user information — just the minimum needed to run a functioning card bot.

### § 02 · Who This Policy Applies To

- 👤 **Card Grabbers** — Anyone who claims a card in any server where the Bot runs
- 🖥️ **Server Owners/Admins** — Anyone who's added the Bot to their server
- 💬 **Official Group Members** — Anyone in the Official Dog Cards Discord Group
- 🤖 **All Bot Interactions** — Anyone using a command, triggering a spawn, or engaging the Bot at all

### § 03 · Data We Collect

> 📊 This list is **complete**. Nothing beyond these five categories — no hidden data, no secret profiles.

| Data Type | Why We Collect It | Retention |
|---|---|---|
| Discord User ID | Track card inventory ownership | While active / until opt-out |
| Discord Username | Console logs for operational awareness | While active / until opt-out |
| Card Grab Timestamps | Prove who grabbed a card first | While card is in inventory |
| Card Inventory Records | Store & display your collection | While active / until opt-out |
| Bot Usage Activity | Console logs of active usage | Temporary / session-based |

### § 04 · Data We Never Collect

| Never Collected | Why |
|---|---|
| Private Messages / DMs | No API access without explicit permission, and we don't want them |
| Passwords / Auth Tokens | Never asked for, never accessible |
| Financial Information | Dog Cards is free — no billing data exists |
| Real Name / Identity | Only your Discord username & ID, nothing more |
| IP Address / Location | Never collected, logged, or stored |
| Server Channel Messages | We don't read or store general chat |
| Email Addresses | Private and inaccessible via the API |
| Anything Discord TOS Prohibits | If Discord says no, we don't do it — period |

### § 05 · How We Use Your Data

- 🎴 **Inventory Management** — Maintains your card collection
- ⚖️ **Dispute Resolution** — Timestamps + IDs power the proof system
- 🖥️ **Operational Monitoring** — Console logs help catch technical issues fast
- 🚫 **Nothing Else** — Never used for ads, profiling, or third-party-sold analytics

### § 06 · Console Logging Explained

Your username/ID may appear in the Bot's console output — internal, visible only to the team, never published. Think *"website access log,"* not *"public record."*

> ✅ **After Opt-Out:** Your name and ID become completely invisible in console output after the next Bot restart. The team will never know you're using the Bot.

### § 07 · Data Storage & Security

- 🔒 **Secure Storage** — Card inventory & User IDs live in a secured database, access restricted to the dev team
- ⏱️ **Minimal Retention** — Data persists only while you're active; opted-out data is deleted on next restart
- 🚫 **No Third-Party Storage** — No analytics services, ad platforms, or data brokers involved
- ⚠️ **Breach Notice** — Affected users notified via the Official Group as promptly as possible

### § 08 · Data Sharing & Third Parties

> We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third party for commercial purposes. Full stop.

**✅ We Share With**
- Nobody, for commercial purposes
- Law enforcement *(only if legally required)*
- Discord *(standard API operations)*

**❌ We Never Share With**
- Advertisers or marketing companies
- Data brokers or analytics firms
- Other Discord bots or services
- Any entity for profit or trade

> ⚖️ **Legal Disclosure:** In rare cases (e.g. a valid court order), we disclose only the legal minimum and notify affected users where permitted.

### § 09 · Your Rights & Opt-Out

- 👁️ **Right to Know** — Ask what we hold; we'll tell you exactly
- 🗑️ **Right to Deletion** — Full permanent deletion, honored on next restart
- 🚪 **Right to Opt Out** — Become fully invisible to the Bot and team
- 🔄 **Right to Opt Back In** — Resume anytime; data collection starts fresh

### § 10 · Opt-Out Process in Detail

1. **Open a support ticket** in `#support` on the Official Dog Cards Discord server
2. **Team acknowledges your request** and confirms what data is currently associated with your account
3. **You acknowledge the trade-off** — no future grab-proof, disputes can't be data-resolved in your favor
4. **Data permanently removed** on the next Bot restart
5. **You're invisible going forward** — still usable, zero data stored

> ⚠️ **Acknowledgement Required:** By opting out, you confirm: (1) no timestamp data for future grabs, (2) disputes can't be data-resolved in your favor, (3) you accept this willingly. We will honor your request 100% — we just want it to be an informed choice.
