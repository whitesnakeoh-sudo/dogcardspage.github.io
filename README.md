## ✨ What is Dog Cards?

> A fun, collectible card bot for Discord communities built around a love of dogs. Cards spawn in eligible channels, and the **first person to claim one owns it**. Simple, fast, and fair.

| 🎴 Feature | 💬 Description |
|---|---|
| **First-Grab Ownership** | Whoever grabs a card spawn first is the rightful owner — verified by timestamped logs |
| **Server Autonomy** | Server owners set their own rules (trading, stealing, gifting — their call) |
| **Proof System** | All disputes resolved using objective, timestamped server-side data |
| **Privacy Respecting** | Minimal data collection, full opt-out support, zero monetization of your info |

### § 03 · Core Rules — Card Grabbing

> 🏆 **The Golden Rule:** *The first person to claim a card owns that card.*

| Rule | Summary |
|---|---|
| 🏆 **First-Grab Ownership** | Timestamps are the definitive source of truth in all disputes |
| 🚫 **No Card Stealing** | Prohibited in the Official Group via exploit, hack, or manipulation |
| ⚡ **Fair Competition** | No bots, scripts, or automation — everyone gets an equal shot |
| 🔍 **Proof-Based Disputes** | Verbal claims aren't enough — server logs decide |

> ⚠️ **Important:** Trying to claim someone else's card — or force a trade through intimidation or exploitation — is a Terms violation regardless of intent.

### § 06 · Proof & Dispute System

All grabs are timestamped and tied to Discord User IDs — **objective data**, no subjectivity. To dispute a grab: open a ticket in `#support` with the card name, approximate time, and your username.

> ⚠️ **Opt-Out Limitation:** If you've opted out of data collection, there's no timestamp proof available for your grabs — meaning disputes involving your account may not be resolvable in your favor. You must acknowledge this trade-off before opting out.
